[COLOR dodgerblue][B]Version 2.1.85. Initial Release[/B][/COLOR]
[I]Where it all began.[/I]
- FlickList integration. Track your watching history across all your devices.
- Device code authentication. Scan QR or enter code at flicklist.tv/link.
- Real-time scrobbling. Playback start, pause, resume, and stop events synced automatically.
- Watchlist and favorites sync with your FlickList account.
- Watched indicators. Checkmarks on movies and shows you have finished.
- In Progress and Jump Back In. Pick up where you left off.
- Browse Similar added to Movie/TV Show context menus and Extras window.
- Because You Watched recommendations powered by your history.
- Search, Discover, People, and My Lists from FlickList.
- Full FenLight FL branding throughout settings and menus.
- Works alongside original FenLight AM. Both can be installed simultaneously.
